1. Dubai Trade Awarded ISO Certification
Dubai Trade has been awarded the International Organisation for Standardisation (ISO) 29990:2010 Learning Services certificate for non-formal education and training.
Dubai Trade, as the premier cross-border trade facilitator, is now among the few business entities in the region to have been successfully inspected and tested for the certification, which recognises the quality of in-house training, life-long learning and vocational courses offered to employees at its service centres. The award is the latest in Dubai Trade's international accolades, which include the ISO 10001:2007, ISO 10002:2004, and ISO 10003:2007 certifications.
